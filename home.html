THE WEAVER BIRD

So be it that on our youth group PRESBYTERIAN WEAVERS OF KENYA is formed with the full intention of acting and working like the weaver bird kingdom.Our constitution is well laid out towards achieving success of the youth communities within and across our motherland Kenya.

MISSION

To improve the members living standard through creation of employment and to reduce immorality and inappropriate behaviors within the members and other community members

VISION

Eradication of poverty through economic empowerment to both the members and other community members and to lodge a campaign against inacceptable traditions among the community.
